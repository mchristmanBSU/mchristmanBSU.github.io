Books

  • How Languages Work: An Introduction to Languages and Linguistics, edited by Carol Genetti with contributing authors
  • The Sound of Language: An Intrduction to Phonetics and Phonology, by Elizabeth C. Zsiga
  • Exploring Language Structure, by Thomas E. Payne
  • Understanding Sentence Structure: An Introduction to English Syntax, by Christina Tortora
  • An Introduction to Linguistic Typology, by Viveka Velupillai
  • Corpora in Applied Linguistics, by Susan Hunston
  • Practical Corpus Linguistics, by Martin Weisser
  • The Oxford Guide to Practical Lexicography, by B.T. Sue Atkins and Michael Rundell
  • HTML: Comprehensive Concepts and Techniques (Fourth Edition), by Gary B. Shelly, Thomas J. Cashman, Denise M. Woods, and William J. Dorn (Part of the Shelly-Cashman Series. It may be out of print, and associated files for projects may be hard to find, but the concepts are applicable and easy to follow for your own projects.)
  • Teach Yourself XML in 24 hours, by Michael Morrison
